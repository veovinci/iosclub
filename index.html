GT iOS Club is a club dedicated to building a community of iOS Developers here at Georgia Tech. Come learn how to make iOS apps! We meet Tuesdays at 6:30 PM in CCB 053!

Below are some designs and apps I've made while apart of this club.

Direction

This is a simple iOS app that tells direction. It vibrates the iPhone when the internal compass is pointed North.

Waves

WAVE is an audio streaming service that allows you to stream your own music, giving you the ability to listen to your music, whatever genre or audio file it is, wherever and whenever.

Atlas

Atlas is a transit app that aims to provide a better, more visual experience finding trains in the areas serviced by Marta. It uses location based services to provide you with the most relevant tranist infromation, such as where the nearest train station is and what time the train will arrive.
